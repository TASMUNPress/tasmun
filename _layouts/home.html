Our message

The TASMUN experience moves beyond simply debating and lobbying to one of true global awareness and knowledge of international relations. This year, we will continue with our Afghanistan Drive Initiative, fundraising for the HELA organization in Afghanistan, which works to spread Model United Nations to Afghan teenagers. We will continue our efforts to encourage and promote cross-cultural dialogue between Afghan students and participants in TASMUN, dialogue that is integral to our international community today. It is our hope that in doing so, participants in TASMUN may gain a greater awareness and connection to the problems that plague our world today.
