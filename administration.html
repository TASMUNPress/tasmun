Transportation

Travel between TAS and the Taoyuan International Airport may take approximately 1.5 hours. A taxi ride should cost approximately NT$1,300. If you are planning to arrive by car to the conference, please note that TAS does not offer any parking space for visiting guests. However, the nearest public parking lot is located at the intersection of Chung Shan North Road and Tien Mou Road, just a five minute walk away from campus.

About TASMUN

The Taipei American School Model United Nations (TASMUN) is a junior level conference designed for aspiring delegates from 7th grade to 10th grade new to Model United Nations. It was first founded as Middle School Taiwan Model United Nations (MS TAIMUN) in 2009 as the junior level affiliate of the Taiwan Model United Nations (TAIMUN), which is a local conference that takes place annually at the American School in Taichung (AST). TASMUN has grown from a local conference consisting of less than a hundred people to more than 300 participants from various countries across asia. In 2013, the TASMUN conference achieved “Highly Recommended” status from The Hague International Model United Nations (THIMUN) Advisory Board. We look forward to seeing you at our next conference in late April!

Conference Payments

The student conference fee for TASMUN 2017 will be 2500 NTD (80 USD) per delegate and advisor. This will include three meals (two lunches and one banquet dinner), t-shirt, and cover our conference expenses. If you are an overseas school we will also arrange your airport transportation. Please email our conference director if you have an interest in arranging any tours while in Taipei. We can provide you with further information and the additional cost.
