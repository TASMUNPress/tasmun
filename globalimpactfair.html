Our Mission: “Create and sustain impact for future leaders at TASMUN!”

There are many creative ways to bring a higher level of impact and awareness about the SDGs to our Model UN program - as we make conscious decisions and changes. This year we are introducing a brand new activity - the “Global Impact Fair”!
We will be hosting a fair (under COVID-19 regulations) to present some efforts that had been made prior to the conference. We are accepting all projects - the criterias are as shown in the digital submission form.
