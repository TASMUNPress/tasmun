The Secretariat welcomes you to TASMUN!

Si Yun E.

Secretary General

It is my absolute privilege to serve as your Secretary-General this year. TASMUN IX will serve as my 17th conference, circling back to where I started my very first MUN experience. Both my middle school and high school day to day commitments have and still continue to exemplify my active involvement with Model UN because of my passion for global politics and our footprint in the world. As such, this year’s conference theme, MUN Impact, aims to shift the focus of MUN to taking real-life actions, which we hope will have a positive ripple effect. My goal is to revolutionize the purpose of MUN, starting from the initiative to transition the focus of our work to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. You will see that our issues and committees this year are solely based on different SDG themes and targets. My team and I are thrilled to have you join us at TASMUN this year, and hope to bring you a uniquely different Model UN experience. As preparations for the conference gear up, I am more than enthusiastic to see you in April 2018! I would like to invite you to come up and speak to me any time during the conference and would be delighted to share my experiences or answer any questions you may have. A warm welcome to the TASMUN 2018 family, I hope to see you soon!
