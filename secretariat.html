Angela

Welcome to TASMUN X! I am Angela Wang, a junior at TAS, and I will be serving as your Deputy Secretary General for Fundraising. When I began my MUN journey in seventh grade, I was timid and shy, just like many others. Nevertheless, debating about past successes and failures has made me realize that we should work beyond proposing solutions on paper, but to create a greater impact for our future amidst the unprecedented difficulties. For this year’s TASMUN, we are focusing on Sustainable Development Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Development. Cindy

Deputy Secretary General

My name is Cindy Li, and I am a junior at Taipei American School. I began my MUN journey in 7th grade as a delegate in TASMUN, so it is my absolute honour to come back full circle and serve as the Deputy Secretary General for Chair Training this year. I have had a fulfilling MUN experience, participating in conferences all over Europe and Asia over the years. Dhirpal

Deputy Secretary General

Hello and welcome to TASMUN X! My name is Dhirpal Shah, a junior at Taipei American School, and I will be serving as Deputy Secretary General for Delegate Mentorship at this conference. One of the best things about TASMUN – and the MUN program as a whole – is the cycle of taking and giving back. As a middle schooler, I learned many things from high school students who had taken their time to come teach me about the world of MUN. Fortunately, I have been able to continue with this program to the point in which I am now able to give back to delegates in the form of mentorship. This year at TASMUN, we are striving to focus on Goal 11 of the Sustainable Development Goals: Sustainable Cities and Communities. Taipei itself is a brilliant example of an efficient and sustainable city - the education system is exemplar and the public transportation in this city is second to none. It was these developments that allowed Taipei to develop so rapidly as a city, so this year, I urge delegates to think outside the box to come up with solutions that would develop cities across the world just as fast. This is what we are looking for at TASMUN X – delegates that want to constantly improve every city in every country. See you there!

Greetings, I'm Emily Hsu and it's my honor to be serving as your Deputy Secretary-General of Administration and Page Coordination at TASMUN X. MUN has played a huge role in my life in not only opening up my perception of the world, but also understanding the importance of speaking up for those whose voices are suppressed. Today’s rapidly developing world creates countless opportunities, but also irreversible consequences. Although fear may be the predominating emotion when faced with so many new challenges, we must step forth with courage and a vision to create a more sustainable future.
