--- layout: split title: Committees and Issues ---

Our diverse committees provide a wide range of options for delegates to discover the impact they can make on our world.

GA1: DISEC (Disarmament & International Security)

  • Addressing the threat of chemical and biological weapons
  • Measures to enforce the sustainable disposal of radioactive waste
  • Question of the prevention of an arms race on the seabed and the ocean floor

GA3: SOCHUM (Social, Cultural and Humanitarian)

  • Measures to combat international modern-day slavery
  • Addressing inequalities caused by the Novel Coronavirus
  • Measures to improve the availability of public healthcare

GA4: SPECPOL (Special Political and Decolonization Committee)

  • Combating Violence and Discrimination Against Women
  • The question of peaceful uses of outer space
  • Measures to promote sustainable foreign investment in formerly colonized states

HRC (Human Rights Council)

  • Measures to eradicate discrimination and persecution based on sexual orientation and gender identity
  • Measure to combat systemic racism and prejudice in criminal justice systems worldwide
  • Promoting the protection of human rights defenders in conflict and post-conflict regions

ECOSOC 1(Economic and Social Council)

  • Addressing the economic problems related to the Novel Coronavirus
  • Measures to strengthen global resilience against virus outbreaks and health pandemics
  • The question of a one-world currency

ECOSOC 2(Economic and Social Council)

  • Addressing the economic problems related to the Novel Coronavirus
  • Measures to strengthen global resilience against virus outbreaks and health pandemics
  • The question of a one-world currency

UN World Health Assembly (WHA)

  • Measures to ensure efficient and equal distribution of COVID-19 vaccines
  • Measures to prevent worldwide pandemics
  • Impact of COVID-19 on refugee camps

Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN)

  • The question of the South China Sea territorial dispute
  • The question of dam-building on the Mekong River
  • Measures to combat corruption and social disparities among ASEAN communities

Advisory Panel Question (APQ) on Economic Inclusion

  • The question of post-incarceration financial security for criminal offenders
  • The question of diversifying developing economies to reduce economic inequality
  • The question of asylum seekers’ right to work

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

  • The Situation in Ethiopia
  • The Situation in Venezuela
  • The Situation in Kashmir

UN4MUN Commission on Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice (CCPCJ)

  • The question of human trafficking in the maritime industry

UN4MUN UN World Tourism Organization (WTO)

  • The question of rebuilding tourism industries harmed by the COVID-19 pandemic

UN4MUN United Nations Children’s Fund (UNICEF)

  • Addressing child and adolescent mental health

UN4MUN United Nations Environmental Programme (UNEP)

  • The effects of hazardous electronic waste on the environment
